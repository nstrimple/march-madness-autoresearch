How do I find my CBS Pool Standings URL?
  1. Log in to cbssports.com
  2. Click Fantasy & Games → College Basketball → Bracket Games
  3. Click your pool name
  4. Click the Standings tab
  5. Copy the URL — it looks like:
    cbssports.com/collegebasketball/brackets/pools/12345678/standings/
How do I find my ESPN Group URL?
  1. Log in to espn.com
  2. Go to Tournament Challenge (under Games)
  3. Click your group name in the left panel
  4. Copy the URL from your browser — it should contain groupID=:
    fantasy.espn.com/tournament-challenge-bracket/2026/en/group?groupID=XXXXXXX
How do I find my bracket's name?
On the standings/group page, look for your name in the list. It might be your name, a nickname, or a team name you chose when you created the bracket. Copy it exactly as it appears.
What does this tool show me?
It finds every game left in the tournament where your pick is different from what others in your pool picked. Those are the games that can swing your standings the most.

For example: if you picked Duke to win the Elite 8 but 7 of 8 others picked Kentucky — that's a high-leverage game. Duke wins → you jump ahead. Kentucky wins → you fall behind.
