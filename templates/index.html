How do I find my Pool Standings URL?
  1. Log in to cbssports.com
  2. Click Fantasy & Games → College Basketball → Bracket Games
  3. Click your pool name
  4. Click the Standings tab
  5. Copy the URL from your browser — it should look like:
    cbssports.com/collegebasketball/brackets/pools/12345678/standings/
How do I find my bracket's name?
On the Standings page, look for your name in the list. The entry name is usually something you set when you created the bracket. It might be your name, a nickname, or a team name. Copy it exactly as it appears.
What does this tool actually show me?
It looks at every game left in the tournament and finds the ones where your pick is different from most people in your pool. Those are the games that can swing your standings the most.

For example: if you picked Duke to win the Elite 8 but 7 out of 8 other people in your pool picked Kentucky — that's a high-leverage game. If Duke wins, you jump ahead. If Kentucky wins, you fall behind.
