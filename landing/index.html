Bracket Tracker

See which remaining tournament games matter most for your bracket — where your picks differ from everyone else in your pool.

Download for Mac

Requires macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later

Setup (takes 2-3 minutes)

1
Unzip the downloaded file. You'll get a BracketTracker folder.
2
Double-click install.command to install. A Terminal window will open and set everything up.
3
Double-click Bracket Tracker.command to start the app. Your browser will open automatically.
macOS security warning? If you see "cannot be opened because it is from an unidentified developer", right-click the file, choose Open, then click Open again in the dialog.
What does this tool do?
It logs into your CBS Sports or ESPN bracket pool, reads everyone's picks, and finds the games where your pick is different from the majority. Those are the games that will swing your standings the most — your high-leverage games.
Is my password safe?
Yes. The app runs entirely on your computer. Your credentials are used only to log in and fetch bracket data — they are never sent anywhere else, never stored, and never logged.
What are the system requirements?
macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later. The installer will download a browser component (~200 MB) on first run. You'll need about 500 MB of free disk space total.
How do I stop the app?
Close the Terminal window that appeared when you launched the app, or press Control+C in that window.
